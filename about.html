About A & O Contracting

Family-owned demolition experts serving Sydney since 2008

Our Story

A & O Contracting was founded in 2008 with a simple mission: to provide Sydney with safe, professional, and affordable demolition services.

What started as a two-person operation has grown into one of Sydney's most trusted demolition contractors. Over the past 15+ years, we've completed thousands of projects ranging from small residential garages to large-scale commercial buildings.

We're a family-owned and operated business, and we treat every project—big or small—with the same level of care and professionalism. Our success is built on three core principles: safety first, quality workmanship, and honest communication.

Today, our experienced team uses state-of-the-art equipment and follows strict safety protocols to deliver exceptional results on every job.
